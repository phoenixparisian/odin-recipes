Carbonara

Traditional Italian Pasta Dish

No cream. No bacon. Simple, yet delicious.

Ingredients

  • 175g guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 100g pecorino romano
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 400g spaghetti (or rigatoni)
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup reserved pasta water

Steps

  1. Cut the Guanciale into 1/5" thick slices then into batons.
  2. Place eggs and yolks in large bowl, whisk to combine. Stir in the pecorino and pepper.
  3. Bring 4 litres of water to boil with the salt. Add pasta and cook as per package directions.
  4. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  5. Cook guanciale in a dry non stick pan over medium heat for 4-5 minutes until golden.
  6. Tip the hot pasta into the pan and toss to coat in the guanciale fat.
  7. Transfer the pasta and any residiual fat into the bowl with the egg mixture. Add 1/2 cup of pasta water and stir with wooden spoon for 1 minute.
  8. Serve immediately in warm bowls. Garnish with extra pecorino and a pinch of black pepper.
